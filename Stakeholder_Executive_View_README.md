# Stakeholder Executive View

## Overview
The Stakeholder Executive View is a strategic communication tool that translates technical NOC data into business value. It is engineered to help operational leaders manage client relationships, track sentiment, and calculate the financial impact of IT service disruptions.

## Key Differentiators
* **Contract-Specific Financial Logic:** The AI assistant is programmed with the exact monthly contract values of key accounts (e.g., BT at $2.1M, Bayer at $1.2M). 
* **Dynamic Risk Calculation:** Instead of generic penalty costs, the system calculates real-time financial risk. For instance, a P1 breach incurs a 0.5% penalty of the specific client's monthly contract value per hour.
* **Executive Protection Protocol:** The system tracks overall stakeholder health (scale of 0-100). If a client's health score drops below 60 (e.g., BT at 58), the AI automatically advises the initiation of protective escalation procedures.
* **Communication Log:** Centralizes the tracking of all executive touchpoints, emails, meetings, and escalation bridges to ensure proactive engagement.

## Business Impact
This module elevates the AIGA platform from a purely technical monitoring tool into a Profit & Loss (P&L) protection system, allowing leadership to prioritize incidents based on revenue risk rather than just technical severity.
