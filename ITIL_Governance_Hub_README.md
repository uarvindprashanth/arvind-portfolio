# ITIL Governance Hub

## Overview
The ITIL Governance Hub provides management-level oversight for Enterprise Service Management (ESM). It is designed for process owners and service delivery managers who need to ensure operational adherence to ITIL frameworks and global compliance standards.

## Core Tracking Metrics
* **Incident & Problem Management:** Monitors active P1/P2 incidents, MTTR trends over 7 days, and Known Error databases.
* **Change Pipeline (CAB):** Visualizes the change register, highlighting high-risk deployments and pending CAB (Change Advisory Board) reviews.
* **SLA Monitoring:** Tracks compliance across P1/P2 response and resolution targets.
* **Audit Readiness:** Displays real-time process maturity scores and tracks readiness for ISO 27001 and SOC 2 Type II audits.

## AI Governance Assistant
Equipped with an integrated AI assistant that holds full context of the current operational posture. The AI can instantly generate CAB review summaries, identify process gaps based on maturity scores, and draft compliance checklists.
